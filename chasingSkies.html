Chasing Skies — IB Thesis Film

For my IB (Inter Baccalaureate) Thesis Film, I worked with three other students to create my final thesis film: Chasing Skies.

Role:

    Cinematographer

Log Line:

    Chasing Skies is a dystopian film that follows the protagonist, Alex, and sister, Tien, as they scavenge the remains of society in an abandoned school.

Process:

    Similar to the “way a writer picks nouns, verbs and adjectives to create meaning on a page”, a cinematographer renders ideas, feelings and actions into visual images through lighting, framing and composition. Fascinated by this role, my main intensions as the cinematographer was to world-build and portay the surrounding desolate environment.

Tools Used:

  • Canon C100
  • Final Cut Pro
